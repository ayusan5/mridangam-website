Legendary Mridangists

Palghat Mani Iyer

A revolutionary mridangist known for elevating the instrument to solo prominence in Carnatic concerts.

Umayalpuram K. Sivaraman

Celebrated for his precision, tonal clarity, and scientific approach to mridangam acoustics.

Palani Subramania Pillai

Known for powerful gumki techniques and a distinctive tonal depth that influenced generations.

Trichy Sankaran

An international ambassador of Carnatic percussion and educator shaping global appreciation.
